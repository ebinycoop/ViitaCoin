By permanently routing capital obligations, such as housing payments, sustenance, energy, taxation, and vital healthcare services, away from the household, this systemic paradigm induces a perpetual liquidity deprivation, commonly identified as the "paycheck-to-paycheck" cycle.

By tokenizing the act of survival itself, VitaCoin transforms the consumer from an exploited resource for external enterprise into the primary stakeholder of an autonomous economic ecosystem.
