Key Features
1. Smart Expense Rewards
Earn VitaCoin automatically on the things you already pay for. By converting your raw consumer transaction data into an asset class, you receive high-utility matches on unavoidable life costs:
	•	Housing (Rent or Mortgage): A powerful 15% payback matching rate.
	•	Groceries & Fuel Supplies: A premium 8.4% payback matching rate.
	•	Automotive & Insurance Premium Payments: A baseline 5.4% payback matching rate.
	•	Healthcare & Medical Incidents: Programmatically tracked via a tiered protective scale to shield your family.
	•	Cash Withdrawls: A token .04%
2. The HomeBuilder Vault (15% Match)
A dedicated, secure vault where your housing rewards are safely directed. This features a beautiful paradigm shift:
	•	For Renters: It turns high, predatory rent payments into accumulated down-payment equity, helping you buy a primary property of your own.
	•	For Homeowners: It turns a relentless mortgage liability into immediate funding for property enhancements, equity acceleration, or secondary acquisitions.
3. The Life Security Vault
Your everyday rewards (groceries, fuel, insurance) go into flexible, yield-bearing vaults. Subjected to an initial 90-day time-lock, these assets grow over time, creating a reliable, automated safety net for unexpected emergencies and long-term dreams.
4. Hyper-Scarce Tokenomics
Unlike traditional inflationary tokens, VitaCoin features an absolute finite supply cap of exactly 1,444,444,444 $VITA. Every single digit is intentional. This tight structure ensures that as global adoption grows, scarcity naturally drives long-term asset value.
5. Ironclad Security & Sybil Defenses
To protect early stakeholders and investors from fraud or multi-wallet exploitation (commonly known as a Sybil Attack), the protocol utilizes a strict 10 $VITA Token-Gated Entry Gate alongside Plaid biometric and legal identification checking. One living soul, one verified face, exactly one VitaCoin reward pipeline.
