VITA Whitepaper

Abstract

VITA is a real-world asset backed rewards protocol that turns everyday spending into long-term housing and life security through tokenized payback.

Core Mechanism

  • 10% payback on Housing expenses → HomeBuilder Vault
  • 5% payback on Groceries & Fuel → Life Vault
  • Lower rates on other essentials
  • Velocity limits for system stability
